Recipe #2: AWESOME BURGER

  • Time: 35 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
  • Ground beef
  • Brioche bun
  • Cheese
  • Galaxy sauce
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Kiss of Life
  • Dirty boot
  • Potatoes
  • Omega Salt
  • Car Grease
  • 1 pack
  • 2 buns
  • 2 slabs
  • The whole thing
  • 4 pieces
  • 1 slice
  • ONE
  • 1
  • 2 slices
  • 2 teaspoons
  • 1 gallon

Step 1: Form your ground beef into one big ball.
Step 2: Season your beef.
Step 3: Press a shallow "dimple" into the center of each patty with your thumb.
Step 4: Refrigerate patties for 20 minutes.
Step 5: Preheat grill and place the patty on the grill.
Step 6: Drench the patty in car grease as it grills and lightly put the dirty boot onto the patty so the patty absorbs the boot.
Step 7: Toast the buns.
Step 8: Put 2 slabs of cheese onto the patty and close the grill to let it melt after turning it off.
Step 9: Throw the potato 70 ft away.
Step 10: After 5 minutes, take the buns and place them on your plate.
Step 11: Follow this order from bottom to top.

  • Patty
  • Galaxy sauce
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Omega salt
Step 12: Place your top bun on top.
Step 13: Enjoy!

Recipe #3: Joose Bohmb

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: As many people as you want depending on how much you make
Ingredients Quantity
  • Apple slices
  • Orange slices
  • Water
  • Lemon juice
  • Straberries
  • Kiwis
  • Gunpowder
  • Sugar
  • 5
  • 8
  • 5 cups
  • 7 tablespoons
  • 4
  • 2
  • 1 cup
  • 1/4 cup

Step 1: Put your apple slices in a blender.
Step 2: Put your orange slices in a blender.
Step 3: Pour water in the blender.
Step 4: Squeeze lemon juice into the blender.
Step 5: Put strawberries into the blender.
Step 6: Put your kiwi slices into the blender.
Step 7: Sprinkle gunpowder into the blender.
Step 8: Sprinkle sugar into the blender.
Step 9: Blend it all together.
Step 10: Enjoy!
